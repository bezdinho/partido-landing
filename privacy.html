Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Partido is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how it is used.

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the Partido mobile application, website, and related services.

This Policy is established in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including Law No. 09-08, and follows international best practices, including principles inspired by GDPR.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for processing your personal data is:

BBF VENTURES

Email: admin@partido.ma

BBF VENTURES determines the purposes and means of processing your personal data in connection with the Partido application.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • the Partido mobile application
  • the Partido website and landing page
  • all services, features, and functionalities provided through the app
  • communications between you and Partido

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect only data that is necessary to operate and improve Partido.

3.1 Account and Authentication Data

You may create an account using:

  • email and password
  • phone number (OTP verification)
  • Google login
  • Apple login
  • Facebook login

Depending on the method used, we may process:

  • name or username
  • email address
  • phone number
  • authentication identifiers provided by third-party services

3.2 Profile Data

You may provide or make available the following profile information:

  • profile photo
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • city
  • football level
  • preferred position
  • language
  • optional biography

Some profile information may be optional.

3.3 Activity and Usage Data

We process data related to your activity on Partido, including:

  • matches created, joined, or left
  • match details (time, location, participants)
  • participation history
  • in-app interactions

3.4 Messaging Data (Match-Based Chat Only)

When you participate in a match:

  • you are automatically included in a group chat linked to that match
  • messages you send are visible to all participants in that match
  • when you leave the match, you lose access to the associated chat

Partido does not offer private one-to-one messaging at launch.

3.5 Location Data

With your consent, Partido may access your device location to:

  • determine your city
  • show nearby matches

You can refuse or disable location access at any time and manually select a city.

3.6 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • device type and operating system
  • app version
  • IP address
  • language settings
  • logs and diagnostic data (including crash reports)

3.7 Notification Data

We may process:

  • push notification tokens
  • notification preferences

3.8 Support and Safety Data

When you contact support or report an issue:

  • content of your request
  • relevant account data
  • contextual information necessary to handle the request

4. How Data Is Collected

Your data is collected:

  • directly from you
  • automatically through your use of the app
  • from your device (subject to permissions)
  • from third-party authentication providers

5. Purposes of Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

5.1 Provision of Services

  • account creation and management
  • match organization and participation
  • enabling match-related interactions

Legal basis: contractual necessity

5.2 Platform Improvement

  • personalization
  • feature optimization
  • performance improvement

Legal basis: legitimate interest

5.3 Security and Moderation

  • detection of abuse, fraud, or misconduct
  • handling reports
  • enforcement of platform rules

Legal basis: legitimate interest

5.4 Communication

We may use your personal data to:

  • send service-related messages and account notifications
  • respond to support requests and reports
  • send promotional emails, SMS messages, and push notifications about Partido, matches, tournaments, offers, updates, or related content, where permitted by applicable law

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time using the unsubscribe option provided in the message, your device settings where applicable, or by contacting us.

Legal basis: contractual necessity / legitimate interest / consent where required

5.5 Technical Operation

  • maintenance
  • debugging
  • performance monitoring

Legal basis: legitimate interest

5.6 Legal Compliance

  • compliance with applicable laws
  • response to legal requests

Legal basis: legal obligation

5.7 Optional Features

  • location services
  • non-essential notifications

Legal basis: consent

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers strictly for the purpose of operating Partido.

These may include:

  • infrastructure and database providers
  • authentication providers (Google, Apple, Facebook)
  • real-time communication and messaging service providers
  • technical service providers supporting the operation of the app
  • legal or regulatory authorities where required

These providers process data only to the extent necessary to deliver their services.

Your personal data is never sold.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside Morocco by third-party service providers.

In such cases, BBF VENTURES ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, which may include standard contractual clauses, user consent, or authorization from the competent authority, in accordance with Law No. 09-08.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data as follows:

  • Account data: retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 60 days of account deletion
  • Match and activity data: retained for the duration of your account
  • Technical logs and diagnostic data: retained for up to 12 months
  • Support requests: retained for up to 24 months after resolution

Certain data may be retained beyond these periods where required by law or for the resolution of disputes.

9. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • request deletion of your personal data
  • object to certain processing for legitimate reasons
  • request restriction of certain processing, where applicable
  • request a copy of the personal data you have provided to us, where applicable
  • withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • request information about how your personal data is collected, used, retained, and shared

You may exercise your rights by contacting us at: admin@partido.ma

You may also lodge a complaint with the competent authority, including the CNDP in Morocco.

Partido does not generally use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

10. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account through the app or by contacting us.

Certain data may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, security, or dispute resolution.

11. Security

BBF VENTURES implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

12. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of your account and login methods.

If you share access to your account with another person, you may remain responsible for actions taken through your account. If you believe that your account has been accessed without authorization or that any login method linked to your account has been compromised, you should contact us immediately at admin@partido.ma.

We encourage users to choose strong passwords and to keep their login credentials secure.

13. Age Requirement

Partido is intended for users aged 14 years and older.

If we become aware that an account does not meet this requirement, we reserve the right to take appropriate action.

14. Disclosure to Other Users and Third Parties

Partido enables users to participate in matches and related group chats. Information that you choose to share within a match, a group chat, or any other user-facing area of the app may be visible to other users participating in that context.

If you disclose information to other users or to third-party services linked from Partido, different privacy practices may apply to that information. Partido does not control the privacy practices of third parties and is not responsible for their independent policies or actions.

Users should exercise caution when sharing personal information with others through the app.

15. Third-Party Services

Partido relies on third-party services to operate certain features of the application.

These include:

  • authentication services (Google, Apple, Facebook)
  • real-time messaging and communication service providers
  • infrastructure and database services
  • technical service providers supporting the operation of the app

These third-party providers may process personal data on our behalf or as independent controllers, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

16. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Partido's website and related digital services may use cookies and similar technologies, such as local storage, pixels, or similar tools, to ensure proper functioning, improve user experience, remember preferences, measure traffic, and support security and performance.

These technologies may be used for purposes such as:

  • enabling essential website functions
  • remembering user preferences
  • improving usability and performance
  • measuring audience and traffic
  • supporting security and fraud prevention

Where required by applicable law, Partido will request your consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

You may manage or disable cookies through your browser or device settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the website or services.

If Partido introduces additional analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

17. Regulatory Compliance

In accordance with Law No. 09-08, BBF VENTURES undertakes to comply with all obligations related to personal data processing, including declaration or authorization with the competent authority where required.

18. Business Transfers

If BBF VENTURES or Partido is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, asset transfer, or similar business transaction, your personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

Where required, we will provide appropriate notice before your personal data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

19. Events, Photos, Videos, and Likeness

Partido may organize tournaments, events, or other in-person activities. In connection with such activities, we may capture photographs, videos, or audio recordings in which participants may appear.

Such content may be used for operational, community, promotional, or communication purposes related to Partido, in accordance with applicable law.

Participants will be informed in advance when such content may be captured. Where applicable, consent will be requested before use for promotional purposes. Participants may contact us at admin@partido.ma to request removal of content in which they appear.

20. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.

The latest version will always be available on the Partido website.

21. Future Use of Analytics and Technical Tools

Partido may introduce additional technical tools in the future to improve the application, such as analytics or performance monitoring tools.

If such tools are implemented and involve the processing of personal data, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and where required, users will be informed or asked for consent.

22. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, the processing of your personal data, the security of your account or data, or if you wish to exercise your rights or request account deletion, you may contact:

BBF VENTURES

General support and privacy requests: admin@partido.ma
